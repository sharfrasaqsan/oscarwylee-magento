Make and Materials

Our sports frames are made from TR90, a thermoplastic material produced through Swiss technology. TR90 is incredibly durable, flexible and lightweight.

These frames also include anti-slip technology in the form of rubber materials to ensure they are secure on your face through all activities.

After your frames have gone through the production process, they undergo cleaning and strict quality control, are individually examined and then packed before making their way to you!
