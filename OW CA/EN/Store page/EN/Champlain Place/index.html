What Treatments or Eye Care Processes Are Offered in the Oscar Wylee Champlain Place Optometrist Location?

Additional tests that may also be performed can include a dilated fundus exam. The treatments or eye-care processes that are done in the Oscar Wylee Champlain Place optometrist location are listed below.

  • A Tonometry Test: An optometrist or optician will conduct a tonometry test which involves the use of a machine that will release small puffs of air into your eyes. This will assist with measuring pressure in the eye.
  • A Digital Photograph: An optometrist or optician may take a digital photograph of your eye which can be kept on record to use in future appointments to evaluate any changes to your eye.
  • Slit Lamp Test: A slit lamp test can be used to evaluate your eye health, by examining your cornea and iris which are the anterior and posterior of the eye.
  • Refraction Test: During a refraction test, an optometrist will have you view an image through different lenses and ask you to confirm which helps you see the image clearer.
  • Dilated Fundus Exam: A dilated fundus exam can help an optometrist assess your health in more detail by getting a clear view of the retina. This test is typically carried out for patients with diabetes and those who may be at risk of developing retinal damage.
