<p class="para-content">While contact lenses can be very beneficial and have advantages over prescription glasses, some limitations should be considered before purchasing this type of eyewear. <li><span class="list-title">Risk of eye infections: </span> Wearing contact lenses can increase a person’s risk of developing an eye infection. Eye infections may be caused by wearing contacts for longer than they are supposed to or not taking proper care of the lenses.</li>
