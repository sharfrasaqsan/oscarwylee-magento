How to Understand Whether You Have a Diamond face or Not?

To find out whether you have a diamond face, you need to measure the face and consider the characteristics of your facial features. To find out the shape of a face when measuring it, there are four main areas to estimate; the forehead, the cheekbones, the jaw line and the length of the face from chin to hairline. If the cheeks are the widest part of your face, with the forehead narrow, the jawline narrow, and the chin tapering to a point, you have a diamond face shape. The features of a diamond face shape are sharp and contoured. The forehead of someone with a diamond shaped face is smaller than a person with a heart shaped face.

To determine if you have a diamond face you should do the following.

  1. Measure the length of your face from the tip of the chin to the centre of the hairline.
  2. Measure the width of your face, across the forehead, the cheeks and the jawline. If your cheeks are the widest part of your face, with your jawline significantly smaller and the forehead also narrow, you have a diamond shaped face.
  3. You should next consider the defining characteristics of a diamond face and if you have these features.
  4. Consider: Is your face sharp and contoured, is your jawline tapered into a point, are your forehead and hairline narrow? If the answer to these questions are yes, you may have a diamond shaped face.
