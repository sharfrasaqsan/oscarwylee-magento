How to Choose the Best Sunglasses for Women?

Content

  • Face shape: A person can use their face shape to choose a sunglasses frame that suits their proportions. A person with a round face can benefit from rectangular and square glasses. Those with an oval face shape look best in square and aviator glasses. Cat-eye and oval glasses suit those with heart-shaped faces. A square face suits round and oval glasses. A person with a pear face benefits from rectangular and oval frames. Finally, a diamond face shape is complemented by oval and round glasses.
