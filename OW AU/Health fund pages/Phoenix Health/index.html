Phoenix Health Fund at Oscar Wylee

As a member of Phoenix Health Fund, you can claim on optical extras using your health fund’s coverage for optical services, such as prescription frames, lenses or glasses. Phoenix Health Fund benefits reset every calendar year, claim your glasses before time runs out! Any extras you don’t use will be lost.

With Oscar Wylee, you can have up to 60% coverage on select optical purchases when you shop with us. Look at the table below to see your Phoenix Health Fund policy’s name and the amount of coverage that policy gives when you shop at Oscar Wylee.

Your coverage might change or not apply to extras such as add-ons, coatings or tinting.
