What Materials are Used to Create Oscar Wylee’s Beren Glasses?

The primary material used to create the Oscar Wylee Beren frame is acetate. Acetate, also known as cellulose acetate, is a bioplastic, made from cotton fibres or wood pulp. At Oscar Wylee, acetate glasses are made by adding a dye to cellulose acetate, which is rolled into a sheet. The shape of the glasses is then cut from this sheet.
