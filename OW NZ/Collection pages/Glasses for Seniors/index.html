Visual field test

Visual Field Testing (VFT) is used to quantify a person’s peripheral vision of each eye individually. This test allows the optometrist to establish a baseline of your eye health and also diagnose eye diseases like glaucoma.

A VFT can be helpful for those with progressive eye diseases, and neurological conditions that affect their visual fields as well as monocular drivers who need to fit certain criteria to continue driving.

Not every Oscar Wylee store offers visual field testing, call your nearest location to find out more.

Retinal Scan

A retinal scan is performed by an optometrist and provides insight into the health of the eye. It also helps diagnose retinal diseases, some of which include:

  • Optic nerve health (including signs of optic neuritis and atrophy)
  • Diabetic retinopathy (a complication from diabetes that causes vision loss due to damaged blood vessels in the eye)
  • Blood vessel health (including hypertension, blockages and leakages)
  • Macular health (including Macular Degeneration)

Not every Oscar Wylee store offers retinal scans, call your nearest location to find out more.

Our Lenses

Oscar Wylee offers a wide range of lenses to serve your visual and lifestyle needs.

Multifocal lenses

Multifocal lenses are a convenient solution for people who are both nearsighted and farsighted. They contain three different prescriptions in one lens—near, intermediate and distance. With multifocals , you have everything you need in one pair, you can read a book, watch tv and go for a drive with ease.

Bifocal glasses

Bifocals are designed for those who struggle with close-up and far vision. The bifocal lens is divided into two defined segments that contain different prescriptions, so you don’t have to swap between glasses.

Reading glasses

Reading glasses are a solution to an age-related eye condition called presbyopia. Readers magnify nearby objects, allowing you to read a book or do close-range activities comfortably, without blurry vision or eye strain.

You may be familiar with over-the-counter reading glasses. These have a preset magnification strength and the same prescription in both lenses, making them less expensive but unable to provide full correction for individuals. That is why we recommend you choose a pair of reading glasses from Oscar Wylee, we make them custom to your face and prescription needs.

High index lenses

High index lenses are designed to bend light more efficiently compared to regular lenses, which allows the lenses to be thinner and lighter, making them more comfortable for everyday use. For people who wear glasses with a strong prescription, it can be beneficial to invest in high index lenses.
