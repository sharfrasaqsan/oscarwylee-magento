When should I get my kid’s eyes tested?

Oscar Wylee offers free eye tests for children under 18. It is recommended that children have an eye exam:

  • When you suspect there may be issues with your child's vision
  • Just before they start school
  • At your optometrist's suggestion

Why do children need regular eye tests?

Clear vision is crucial for your child’s development. Not being able to see clearly can impact how a child learns and grows. It can determine how well they succeed at school, in areas such as reading and writing.

It is important that you get your child’s vision tested if you notice that they are having trouble seeing. Early detection is key with children’s vision and can improve treatment options.

Signs your child might need an eye exam

There are several signs or symptoms that are an indication that your child may benefit from having an eye test. These signs are listed below:

  • Frequent blinking
  • Squinting to see objects
  • Issues with focusing
  • Problems with school or homework
  • Headaches
  • Being overly clumsy
  • Poor handwriting
