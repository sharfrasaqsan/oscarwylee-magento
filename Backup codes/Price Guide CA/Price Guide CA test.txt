<h3>Frame Styles</h3>
<div>
<p>• Acetate</p>
Also known as cellulose acetate, these frames are traditionally hand-crafted by skilled craftsmen, with each frame passing through numerous stages of production and are hand-polished to ensure a glorious lustre. Our collections are a mix of Italian Mazzucchelli and Chinese-crafted plant-based plastic, which is both durable and light-weight.</div>
<div>
<p>• Stainless-Steel</p>
As part of our durable frame family, some of our most popular styles such as Kato, are now in stainless steel. These frames are hypoallergenic, corrosion resistant, light weight, and not sensitive to temperature changes.</div>
<div>
<p>• Titanium</p>
Our titanium frames are durable, corrosion resistant, lightweight, hypo-allergenic, with fixed spring hinges. Much of our titanium is sourced from Japan.</div>
<div>
<p>• TR/Ultem material</p>
We love our Ultem frames! They are super comfortable and very durable. Our Ultem frames are heat resistant, with high dielectric strength and flexible. The material is widely used in medical and chemical instruments. It is currently available in a range of colours for both matte and gloss finish.</div>
