<div class="faqheader"><strong>COVID-19: Orders</strong></div>
<div class="faqcontent">
<div class="faqanswer">
<p><strong>COVID-19: What happens to my order placed before the lockdown?</strong></p>
<p>All orders placed in our stores before the unfortunate, but temporary, restriction changes due to COVID-19 will be held for you.</p>
<p>If you need to collect your order, contact the store of purchase and they will be able to guide you through the best way of you being able to collect your new glasses.</p>

<p>There is currently a slight delay with online orders being processed.&nbsp;Once your order has been processed you will receive an AusPost tracking number.</p>

<div class="faqheader"><strong>Pricing?</strong></div>
<div class="faqcontent">
<div class="faqanswer">
<p>We offer a single pair of prescription glasses from $169 or two pairs from $199. Lens extras such as anti-glare, blue light filter or polarised coatings are available for an additional cost.</p>
<p>Speak with our store team and they can help with pricing and health fund quoting.</p>
</div>

<div class="faqheader"><strong>Eye test price?</strong></div>
<div class="faqcontent">
<div class="faqanswer">
<p>We offer bulk-billed eye tests for eligible Medicare cardholders, so there are no out-of-pocket expenses. Under Medicare, you can claim 1 comprehensive eye test every 3 years. <br><br>If you are not covered under Medicare, eye tests are $60 each.</p>
<p>Click <a href="/booking/">here</a> to book an eye test.</p>
</div>

<div class="faqheader"><strong>Repairs/Returns</strong></div>
<div class="faqcontent">
<div class="faqanswer">
<p><strong>Repairs</strong></p>
<p>If your frames are damaged, head in and see our store teams and they will be able to help fix or replace them for you.</p>
<p>Charges may apply for items not covered under our 12-month warranty.</p>
<p><strong>Returns</strong></p>
<p>Since each pair of glasses is customised based on your prescription, we cannot offer any exchange or refund for change of mind on prescription items.</p>
<p>If the prescription provided from an external optometrist is incorrect or if the prescription is no longer valid, purchasers agree that Oscar Wylee is not responsible for the cost of the remake.</p>
<p>For non-prescription over the counter items, we can offer exchanges within 14 days from received granted they are still in satisfactory condition.</p>
</div>
</div>
